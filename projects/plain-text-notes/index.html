Plain Text Notes

A note-taking approach built around plain text files, simple editors, and long-term portability.

I like systems that still make sense after the excitement fades.

Plain text notes are not flashy, but they are portable, easy to back up, and readable almost anywhere. No lock-in, no heavy setup, no special environment required.

The more tools I try, the more I respect simple formats that survive trends.
