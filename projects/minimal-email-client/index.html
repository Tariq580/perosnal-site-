Minimal Email Client

A keyboard-first, text-heavy email client for people who want email to feel boring again.

project link ↗
projectideacommunication

Most email clients feel overloaded long before the inbox itself becomes the problem.

I keep thinking about a quieter alternative: plain text, keyboard-driven, simple threading, no visual noise, no pressure to turn email into a productivity sport.

I am still sketching the shape of it, but I like the constraint.
