Snapmeme

A meme site built quickly, posted publicly, and mostly ignored. A useful lesson in attention, distribution, and patience.

projectfailureinternet

Snapmeme was one of my early attempts to build something quickly and post it online.

Making it was exciting. Getting people to care was the hard part.

The project failed, but it taught me something important: building is only one part of the job. Distribution, timing, consistency, and knowing who the work is for matter just as much.
