Reading Tracker

A web app idea that reminded me a simple text file often does the job better.

project link ↗
projectreadingpaused

I started building a reading tracker because the idea sounded useful and satisfying.

Halfway through, I realized a plain text list already gave me most of what I actually needed. That made the project less exciting, but the lesson was valuable.

Sometimes the best product decision is to stop building.
