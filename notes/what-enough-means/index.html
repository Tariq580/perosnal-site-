What "Enough" Means

If you never define enough, ambition can quietly become a trap.

noteambitionlife

Most people chase more without deciding what enough would look like.

More money. More followers. More proof. More options.

The problem is not ambition. The problem is having no line that tells you when the pursuit is serving your life and when your life is serving the pursuit.
