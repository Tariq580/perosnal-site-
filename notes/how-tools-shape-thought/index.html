How Tools Shape Thought

Fast tools, slow tools, and the kinds of thinking each one encourages.

notetoolsthinking

Every tool has an opinion about how you should think.

A fast notes app encourages capture. A slower one encourages reflection. A blank editor feels different from a feed, and both produce different kinds of thoughts.

I want to pay more attention to what my tools reward, not just what they let me do.
