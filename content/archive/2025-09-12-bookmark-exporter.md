This idea came from wanting a cleaner relationship with saved links.

Instead of keeping bookmarks trapped inside a browser UI, I wanted a simpler export into plain text so the links could be searched, edited, or moved anywhere.

Small utilities like this do not look glamorous, but they often become the tools I use the longest.
